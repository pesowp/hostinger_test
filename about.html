About AGC, Okpuno

Angel Gabriel College, Okpuno is a new creation of the Catholic Diocese of Awka geared towards offering quality education to the upcoming generation of students with the right tools, skill, and knowledge, moral and mental abilities to excel in life. To be run by very experienced teachers with clear vision, mission and objectives.

It starts this academic year with only Junior Secondary one (Upper Basic 7). We shall offer the subjects meant for Upper Basic classes with some foreign languages.

Get in Touch

Angel Gabriel College is located right inside the premises of St Gabriel's Catholic Parish, Okpuno with serene and conducive environment for learning.

Our Core Values

  •   Pursuing excellence through hard work.
  •   Growth in knowledge through discipline.
  •   Global integrity through God-fearing foundation.

Some of Our Facilities

  •   Adequate instructional materials for all subjects.
  •   Fully equipped school library.
  •   Very experienced, qualified and trained teachers.
  •   Sports and recreational facilities.
  •   Standard multipurpose hall.
  •   Standard and decent toilet facilities.
  •   ICT facilities.

-- Vision --

  • To develop God-fearing leaders.
  • To acquire a set of spiritual and moral values.
  • To uphold the principles of academic excellence.
  • To foster human capital development that provides solution to modern challenges.

-- Motto --
Gratia Dei Floremus (With the Grace of God, We Flourish)

Grace is a gratuitous gift of God bestowed on humans. God's grace is in abundance and we are resolved to cooperate with God to become better humans. When we cooperate positively with the grace of God, we attain great heights in all facets of our existence.

-- Mission --

Angel Gabriel College aims to develop excellent and God-fearing future leaders , with sound academic, moral and solid foundation.

We are in tune with the future of learning, and we continue to lead the pack in world-class education. We inculcate high moral and ethical values in our students as we prepare them for lifelong learning, service and satisfaction.

Gratia Dei Floremus
